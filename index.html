Light-first editorial framework

Quiet surfaces, bold type, and the kind of spacing that lets the page breathe.

PacificCSS is built around the Apple web language found in editorial and developer pages: soft greys, generous type, rounded cards, and restrained color moments that support the hierarchy instead of competing with it.
